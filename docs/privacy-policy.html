Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-17

This policy describes how our applications and tools (“the App”) handle information when interacting with Meta/Facebook and other services.

What we collect

  • Operational data: page/access tokens, post IDs, error logs, timestamps.
  • No end-user personal data: We do not intentionally collect names, emails, or contact info of end users.

How we use data

  • Create and manage posts or automate workflows you explicitly configure.
  • Maintain security, debug issues, and comply with platform policies.

Sharing

We do not sell data. We may share operational data with service providers strictly to run the App (hosting, logging, monitoring), bound by confidentiality.

Retention

Operational data is kept only as long as necessary for the stated purposes, then rotated or deleted. You may request earlier deletion (see below).

Security

Access tokens and secrets are stored in secure storage with restricted access. We rotate credentials periodically.

Your choices

  • You can remove the App’s access in your Facebook settings at any time.
  • You can request deletion of operational data we control (see “Data Deletion”).
