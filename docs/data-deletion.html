Data Deletion

If you want us to delete operational data we control (e.g., access tokens, logs, post IDs), follow one of these steps:

  1. Remove the App from your Facebook settings. Meta may send us an automated deletion request, which we honor.
  2. Email request: send a message to davidjmedellin@gmail.com with subject “Data Deletion Request”. Include:
    • Your Page ID or link
    • Approximate dates of activity
    • What you want deleted (tokens/logs/posts created by the App)

We will confirm receipt and delete eligible data within a reasonable timeframe unless retention is required by law or to resolve security incidents.
